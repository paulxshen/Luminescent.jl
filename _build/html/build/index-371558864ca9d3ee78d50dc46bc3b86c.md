## Overview
Lumi FDTD (finite difference time domain) is open source software for full wave simulations and inverse design in photonics and RF. [Luminescent AI](https://luminescentai.com) is a fabless semiconductor startup designing production grade photonic tensor units for 10x energy efficiency AI compute. We also offer photonics design consulting and have open sourced Lumi FDTD to contribute to the community.
