# Home
## Overview
Lumi FDTD (finite difference time domain) is open source software for full wave electromagnetic simulation and inverse design in photonics and RF. It's maintained by [Luminescent AI](https://luminescentai.com), a Stanford alum founded startup designing photonic tensor cores for >10x energy efficient AI compute. Photonics design consulting is also available.
